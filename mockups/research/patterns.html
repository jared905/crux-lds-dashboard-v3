What this says: In NBA, question-mark titles outperform platform-wide by 24% — about double the platform-wide lift (12%). Numbered lists and emojis also outperform. Game-outcome phrasing underperforms, likely because the result is in the thumbnail anyway, so the title carries no surprise.
